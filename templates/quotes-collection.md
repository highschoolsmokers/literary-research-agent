## Citation Format

Every quote uses this format:

> "[Exact quote from the text]"
>
> — Author, *Title*, Location. Year. **[confidence level]**

Confidence levels:
- **[verified]** — confirmed exact against source text; source excerpt included
- **[high confidence]** — believed accurate with source excerpt, not verified word-for-word against definitive edition
- **[paraphrase]** — captures the idea, may not be exact wording; source excerpt shows the original
- **[unverified]** — attributed to this author but source text not confirmed; no source excerpt available
